What is the Waiter/Waitress program?

  • opportunity for 14-16 year olds to go through real life situations in a workforce.
  • Paid job with responsibility to serve 6 days a week, 3 meals a day.
  • One day off a week which includes out of camp activities
  • Continues ones camper experience.
  • 7-week bonding period with the table you serve.
  • Encourages relationship-building among counselors and campers.

Waitressing for Lunch!

Each meal the waitresses go up at least 40 minutes early to set up the tables, eat, and prepare to serve.

Coaching & Leadership Opportunities

Being one of the eldest divisions in camp means more perks. Including coaching different sports, helping out at activities, and being involved in assisting younger divisions.

Trips & Events

As a waitress, since it is a paid job, each wednesday we get a day off to different places. Some may include the mall, waterpark, amusementpark, and more. In addition, the older divisons go on a big trip towards the end of the summer, this year we went to Baltimore!
